FIRST ENTRY

tuesday, november 11, 2024

hello ! happy zero-th birthday to this blog.

i'm quite nervous to start writing here to be honest. i've never started a diary in my life and i hope i can keep up with this one. i guess i can write about pretty much anything, but i'll try to keep it about somewhat important stuff.

i now started my phd about a year ago, and i'm still not sure about wether i made good progress or not. on one hand i'm already submitting my first paper (to a nice journal yahoo ! if it gets accepted that is), but on the other hand i always feel so much behind. time feels like its slipping so fast and i'm making so little progress each day. writing papers also seems to be one of my weak points (even though my supervisor is quite strict, even she says it), so i guess this blog can be a form a training for the moment. i'm not sure if i like knowing other people can read this, but it does make me feel a bit warmer knowing that i'm sharing this with other people, as if i'm unloading some kind of burden. anyway, i'm going to add more categories to this website, see you soon !
